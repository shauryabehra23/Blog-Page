📊 1. Blog Platform with Content Moderation
Users can:

Register/login with email

Create/edit/delete their own blog posts

Upload featured images for posts

View other users' published posts

Like/comment on posts

Edit their profile with avatar

Admins can:

Approve/reject posts before publication

Delete any post or comment

Manage user roles (promote to admin/ban users)

View analytics (posts per user, activity logs)

Manage categories/tags
